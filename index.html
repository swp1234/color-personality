      About This Test

      How the Color Personality Test Works

      The Color Personality Test uses a unique 7-step interactive process where you pick colors from a spectrum and choose between palettes. Your instinctive color choices are analyzed to determine your dominant personality color among 8 types: Red (Passionate), Blue (Intellectual), Green (Harmonizer), Yellow (Optimist), Purple (Creator), Orange (Energizer), Pink (Empath), and White (Pure).

      The Science of Color Psychology

      Color psychology studies how colors affect human behavior and emotions. Research shows that color preferences can reflect personality traits, emotional states, and cognitive styles. This test applies these principles in a fun, interactive format to help you discover which color best represents your personality.
